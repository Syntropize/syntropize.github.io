Syntropize v0.1

  • Make Cards

    Make a Card everytime you would make a folder - it is as simple as that. Each card is a WebApp that can fetch content locally or from the internet.

  • Organize the Board

    Organize cards using order, colors and tags. Sort and Filter cards based on their content and properties. Expose structure and discover new relationships.

  • Access your Files

    Cards store your content, just like folders. Click on the FILE menu to see what is stored inside your cards. Open your files in any application - no lock-in.

  • Publish and Share

    Select files in sequence and run commands on them; Combine files using scripts to generate new content. Share by simply providing a copy or link...
